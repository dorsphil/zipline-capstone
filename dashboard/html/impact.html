Operational Impact

This page highlights the geographic and institutional impact of Zipline's delivery operations across Ghana, showing how healthcare facilities and communities are supported.

Reaching the Frontlines: Deliveries by Facility Type

This chart compares the volume of deliveries received by each facility type including CHPS compounds, health centres, hospitals, and other facilities. Approximately 63.4% of all deliveries are directed to CHPS compounds, the most frontline tier of Ghana's health system.

Insight:
This distribution proves Zipline is not just a "hospital supplier" but the primary circulatory system for community-based healthcare, sustaining rural facilities that were previously hard to reach.

The Zipline Ecosystem: Facility Coverage by Type

This chart displays the distribution of healthcare facility types supported by Zipline Ghana.

The dominance of CHPS compounds and health centres is highly significant. These facilities typically operate at the frontline of primary healthcare, often serving rural communities with limited access to large hospitals.

Insight:
This further affirms our point, as seen in the previous chart, our impact on the frontline, the primary healthcare system.

Geographic Footprint: Regions Served

This chart illustrates how deliveries for 2023 were distributed across regions in Ghana, showing a number of 5 regions in total.

Regional differences in volume are a direct response to healthcare need rather than population size.

Insight:
The data shows that Zipline's operations are demand-driven, clearly indicating our operational reach and the regionsreceiving the most support.

Community Impact: District-Level Beneficiaries

This horizontal bar chart illustrates the distribution of delivery volumes across our scope of 22 districts.

Sefwi Wiawso leads with 22.02% of total deliveries, followed by Aowin (14.44%) and Sefwi Akontombra (9.55%).

Insight:
This demonstration of Zipline's reach at the community level also shows how dependent most districts are on our services, making our deliveries, not a convenience, but a necessity.
